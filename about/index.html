About me

Hello! I'm Ivan Bogachev. I'm a UI developer, a man experienced in modern front-end technologies with an education in computer science, music and drawing arts. I have no degrees, I'm a self-taught person. I prefer to think that I'm not a "designer that knows html", I'm a "programmer with design skills".

One of my strengths is ability to create amazing effects using JS, SVG and modern CSS. Some examples are published on this site and you can take a look on sources for free. Also, I share my knowledge by writing articles on Habrahabr and answering the questions on Toster.

My hobby is music. I've been playing guitar for many years and last year I wrote a book about the most popular flamenco forms. Also, I play piano a little and compose music for a chamber orchestra. This year I published two collections of my pieces for piano and for piano and violin. Flamenco and classical music aren't very popular today, but I like this music and continue to play it no matter what.

I'm looking for a part-time job in Moscow. If you need an experienced UI developer to create websites with complex layouts and incredible effects, we could meet and discuss your vacancy.
